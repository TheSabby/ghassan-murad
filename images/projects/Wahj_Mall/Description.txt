As Ludwig Mies van der Rohe has stated ‘Less is more’. The Seagram Building located in Park Avenue is a reflection of simplicity and integrity in form and structure. Wahj Mall combines the international style through Mies’ modern stylistic movement and the luxury depicted in the of simplicity of design, for the customers to be in an environment that would amplify the ultimate shopping experience. The virtual work involved the creation of the brand, and the signage inside the building.
