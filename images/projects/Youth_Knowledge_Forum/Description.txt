Knowledge Youth Forum is an annual activity presented by Majdi Bukhari International that takes place in Al-Madinah in Saudi Arabia. The forum promotes several subjects to the youth in different fields, including Mobile Applications, Cloud Computing, Virtual Reality and 3D printing. It also focuses on the evolving the Information Technology sector in Al-Madinah in regard to shifting the IT community and covers the most modern practices in the field. The work involved the creation of the event brand, advertising concepts and posters, in addition to the design elements inside the forum.
